2. Services Overview

BrandGEO provides AI Visibility Intelligence services that monitor, measure, and report on a brand's presence across major AI engines. The service includes:

  • Structured query execution across five AI engines using live web search where available
  • Weekly collection, analysis, and scoring of AI engine responses
  • AI Visibility Score (0–100) across six measurement dimensions
  • Per-engine status reporting (KNOW, PARTIAL, MISSING) and position tracking
  • Prioritised Fix This action hub (P0/P1/P2 recommendations)
  • Competitor comparison tracking
  • Access to the BrandGEO client dashboard at app.getbrandgeo.com
  • Strategy calls as specified per plan

BrandGEO is a fully managed service. Clients are not required to install software, access APIs, or perform technical configuration. All service delivery is handled by BrandGEO on the client's behalf.

Note: BrandGEO monitors AI engine outputs as they appear at the time of query. We do not guarantee any specific AI engine behaviour, mention frequency, or score outcome, as AI engine responses are determined by third-party systems outside our control.

3. Free Audit Plan

What is included

The Free Audit is a one-time, no-cost snapshot of a brand's AI Visibility. It includes:

  • One-time query across all five AI engines
  • AI Visibility Score (0–100)
  • Per-engine status (KNOW / PARTIAL / MISSING)
  • Top 3 improvement areas
  • Basic competitor comparison (1–2 competitors)

The Free Audit does not include weekly monitoring, dashboard access, the full Fix This action hub, or strategy calls. These are available under the paid plans.

Eligibility & refresh policy

The Free Audit is available once per Brand Domain. The same brand domain may request a new Free Audit after a minimum of six (6) calendar months from the date the previous audit was delivered.

BrandGEO reserves the right to decline Free Audit requests that appear to circumvent this policy (e.g., through submission of slight variations of the same brand domain or multiple requests from the same organisation).

Delivery

Free Audit results are delivered within 48 business hours of the request being confirmed. BrandGEO will contact you by email to the address provided in the request form.

No obligation

Requesting a Free Audit does not create any obligation to purchase a paid plan. There is no credit card required and no automatic subscription activation.
