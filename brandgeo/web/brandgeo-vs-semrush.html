● The Honest Version

Where Semrush AI Toolkit genuinely wins

  • One bill if you're already a Semrush customer — keyword tracking, backlinks, content tools, and AI visibility together instead of a separate subscription.
  • Backed by years of existing SEO data — rank tracking and backlink history a standalone AI-visibility tool simply doesn't have.
  • A large, established user base already inside the Semrush ecosystem, with the platform trust that comes with it.
  • Reports tie directly into existing SEO workflows — Prompt Research and AI Search Site Audit sit next to the keyword and content tools your team may already use daily.

Where BrandGEO genuinely wins

  • Claude included as standard from Growth up — Semrush gates Claude, Copilot, Grok, DeepSeek, and Meta AI behind a custom-priced Enterprise tier.
  • A real managed-service tier — Semrush AI Toolkit is self-serve software only, with no done-for-you equivalent at any price.
  • Purpose-built for AI visibility, not a feature bolted onto a much larger legacy SEO suite you may not need or already use elsewhere.
  • No per-prompt or per-domain metered add-ons to track — Growth's 150 prompts and 5 engines are simply included, not billed line by line.

Is Semrush AI Toolkit the same price as BrandGEO?

At the entry level they land close — Semrush's standalone AI Visibility Toolkit add-on is $99/month per domain (25 tracked prompts), and BrandGEO Essentials is €99/month. The gap opens up as you scale: Semrush's closest equivalent to BrandGEO Growth is the Semrush One Pro+ bundle at $299/month, but that pays for the full SEO Toolkit plus AI visibility together, not AI visibility alone — and it caps out at around 100 tracked prompts with no Claude coverage. BrandGEO Growth is €299/month for AI visibility specifically, with 150 prompts and all 5 core engines, including Claude, included as standard.

Do I need Semrush's SEO Toolkit to use its AI Visibility features?

Not strictly — Semrush sells the AI Visibility Toolkit as a standalone $99/month per-domain add-on. But the better per-prompt pricing and deeper reporting only show up inside the Semrush One bundle (Starter, Pro+, Advanced), which packages the AI toolkit together with the full SEO Toolkit — so most of the value case assumes you also want, and pay for, the broader suite. BrandGEO doesn't require adopting any other product to get full AI visibility monitoring.

Does Semrush AI Toolkit track Claude the way BrandGEO does?

Not on standard plans. Semrush's AI Visibility Toolkit and Semrush One tiers cover ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google's AI Overviews/AI Mode as standard, with Gemini added on higher tiers — Claude, Microsoft Copilot, Grok, DeepSeek, and Meta AI are reserved for Semrush's custom-priced Enterprise tier. BrandGEO includes Claude as one of its 5 standard engines from the Growth plan up, with no separate enterprise unlock required.
