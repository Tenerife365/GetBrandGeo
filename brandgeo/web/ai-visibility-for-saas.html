AI Visibility for SaaS Companies: How to Get Recommended by ChatGPT, Gemini & Perplexity

SaaS buyers used to Google "best CRM software" and scroll a list of blue links. Increasingly, they ask ChatGPT or Perplexity "what's the best CRM for a 10-person sales team" and take whatever answer comes back — often without ever visiting a search results page at all. Getting named in that answer is quickly becoming as important as ranking #1 organically once was, and it runs on a different set of signals entirely.

How is AI Visibility different from SEO for a SaaS company?

SEO optimizes for ranking in a list of blue links a buyer still has to click through and compare. AI Visibility optimizes for being the product an AI engine actually names in its answer when someone asks a question like "best CRM for a small sales team" — a ranking-vs-recommendation distinction with real mechanical differences. Full breakdown in GEO vs SEO: The Fundamental Difference.

Which AI engines matter most for SaaS buyer research?

It varies by engine. Perplexity tends to cite brands at a higher rate on commercial queries than ChatGPT or Gemini, while Gemini leans more heavily on a brand's own domain content when deciding what to cite. Most SaaS companies need visibility across all of them, since buyers don't consistently pick just one tool — see Perplexity vs Gemini: Which AI Cites Brands Most? for the engine-by-engine breakdown.
