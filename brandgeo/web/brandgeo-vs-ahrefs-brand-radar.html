● The Honest Version

Where Ahrefs Brand Radar genuinely wins

  • No new vendor if you already pay for and trust Ahrefs — AI-citation data shows up right next to the SEO data your team already uses daily.
  • Maps the full funnel — ties AI citations back to Ahrefs' own backlink, content, YouTube, Reddit, and TikTok data, a correlation most standalone GEO tools can't offer.
  • A massive underlying prompt database — 405M+ search-backed prompts derived from real People Also Ask data, not a fixed prompt list.
  • Unlimited domains — analyze as many brands or competitors as you want with no per-brand pricing tier.

Where BrandGEO genuinely wins

  • No second product to buy — a standalone GEO platform, not a $199–699/month add-on stacked on top of an existing SEO suite subscription.
  • Purpose-built for AI visibility — a 6-dimension AI Visibility Score, sentiment tracking, and AI-generated "Fix This" recommendations designed for AI citations specifically, not adapted from SEO tooling.
  • A real managed-service tier — Brand Radar is self-serve analytics only, at any price; BrandGEO's Managed and Pro tiers hand the strategy and reporting work to a team.
  • Claude included — Ahrefs doesn't track Claude at all; BrandGEO does, as one of its 5 core engines.
  • Real geo-context per market and a multi-language sentiment lexicon, built for brands operating across multiple markets and languages.

Is BrandGEO cheaper than Ahrefs Brand Radar?

Ahrefs Brand Radar isn't sold on its own — it requires an active Ahrefs base subscription (Starter from $29/mo, though custom prompt tracking needs the Lite plan at $129/mo) plus the Brand Radar add-on itself: $199/month for a single AI platform index, or $699/month for all platforms bundled. That puts a realistic minimum around $828/month before any custom-prompt overages. BrandGEO Essentials starts at €99/month with core AI-visibility tracking included, and Growth (€299/month) adds all 5 core engines as standard — no per-platform add-on math required.

Do I need to already use Ahrefs to get Brand Radar?

Yes. Brand Radar's AI platform indexes are only sold as an add-on to an existing Ahrefs subscription — there's no standalone way to buy it. If you're not already an Ahrefs SEO customer, that means onboarding onto a full SEO suite just to get AI-visibility tracking. BrandGEO is a standalone, purpose-built product — no existing SEO subscription required.

Which AI platforms does each track, and does either offer a managed service?

Ahrefs Brand Radar covers Google AI Overviews/AI Mode, ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and Copilot (Grok data collection is currently paused, and Claude isn't supported since it requires login) — priced per platform or as an all-platform bundle. BrandGEO's Growth tier and above include all 5 core engines — ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, Perplexity, and Meta AI — as standard, plus a genuinely managed-service option on Managed, Pro, and Enterprise that Ahrefs doesn't offer at any tier; Brand Radar is self-serve analytics only.
