Pricing and features current as of July 2026 — publicly listed prices, verified directly against each provider's own pricing page.

Choose BrandGEO if…

You want the fix done, not just diagnosed

Both platforms will tell you what's wrong with your AI visibility. BrandGEO's Managed and Pro tiers go a step further and have a real team actually fix it — strategy, research, and reporting handled for you, without hiring a separate agency at $1,500–5,000+/month.

Choose Scrunch AI if…

You want deep, self-serve AI-readiness audits

You have an in-house team that wants page-level audits, persona/journey mapping, and technical AI-readiness tooling (AXP), and is comfortable implementing the fixes itself once Scrunch tells you what's missing.

● The Honest Version

Where Scrunch AI genuinely wins

  • Broader platform coverage as standard — 6 platforms including Google AI Mode & AI Overviews on every plan, no tier-gating on which platforms you can track.
  • AXP (Agent Experience Platform) — a technical layer that reformats your site into AI-friendly, token-light pages, something BrandGEO doesn't offer.
  • Persona and journey mapping across AI agents, useful for teams that want to model buyer paths, not just single-prompt mentions.
  • Established customer base and social proof — 500+ companies including Lenovo, Clerk, and Crunchbase, plus a 7-day free trial with no credit card required.

Where BrandGEO genuinely wins

  • A real managed-service tier — Scrunch has no equivalent at any price, even Enterprise; the alternative is a separate $1,500–5,000+/month agency retainer.
  • Lower pricing at every comparable tier — Essentials (€99) undercuts Starter ($300 month-to-month), Growth (€299) undercuts Scrunch's own Growth ($500 month-to-month).
  • All 5 core engines included from Growth up, with no seat surcharge or per-engine add-on to track.
  • One platform across the whole journey — start self-serve on Free or Essentials, move to fully managed on Managed or Pro, without switching vendors.
  • A free one-time audit with no signup required, if you just want a baseline first.
● Common Questions

These are the three questions we hear most when brands are weighing the two. For the full breakdown of how BrandGEO's plans and pricing work, see our complete FAQ.

Is BrandGEO cheaper than Scrunch AI?

Yes, at every comparable tier. BrandGEO Essentials (€99/mo) undercuts Scrunch's entry Starter plan ($300/mo month-to-month, or $250/mo billed annually). BrandGEO Growth (€299/mo) is similarly below Scrunch's Growth plan ($500/mo month-to-month, or $417/mo billed annually). The bigger gap is at the done-for-you end: BrandGEO Managed starts at €900/month for a fully managed service, while Scrunch has no managed-service tier at any price.

Does Scrunch AI offer a managed or done-for-you service like BrandGEO?

No. Scrunch AI is self-serve software across Starter, Growth, and Enterprise — Enterprise adds SSO, an Enterprise Data API, and a "dedicated GTM team," but you and your team are still the ones running audits and implementing fixes. BrandGEO offers that same self-serve path on Free, Essentials, and Growth, plus a genuinely managed option on Managed, Pro, and Enterprise where BrandGEO's own team handles strategy, research, and reporting.

Which AI platforms does each track?

Scrunch AI tracks six platforms as standard on every plan — ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity, Google AI Mode & AI Overviews, and Meta — with prompt, audit, and persona limits that scale by tier. BrandGEO's Growth tier and above include five core engines as standard — ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, Perplexity, and Meta AI — with no per-engine add-on pricing.
