How much does Conductor cost compared to BrandGEO?

Conductor doesn't publish pricing — it's usage-based and quoted per organization after a sales conversation. Industry estimates put a typical Conductor contract between $35,000 and $180,000+ per year, with some large enterprise deployments running upward of $500,000/year. BrandGEO's pricing is public: Free at €0, Essentials at €99/month, Growth at €299/month, Managed at €900/month, Pro from €1,500/month, and Enterprise from roughly €10,000/month for organizations that genuinely need that scale.

Does Conductor offer a self-serve option like BrandGEO?

Not really. Conductor's Essentials tier has a free-trial signup, but every paid plan requires a demo or a sales conversation, and Conductor doesn't offer a done-for-you managed service at any tier — you or your team run the platform yourselves, with a Conductor customer success rep supporting you. BrandGEO offers both ends: self-serve software you can sign up for directly (Free through Growth), and a genuinely managed service (Managed and Pro) where BrandGEO's own team does the work.

Is Conductor overkill for a growth-stage company or SMB?

For most, yes. Conductor is built for large, multi-domain enterprises that already need a full content-creation-plus-technical-SEO-plus-monitoring suite and have budget to match. If what you actually need is to know how ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, and other AI engines talk about your brand — and to fix what's wrong — BrandGEO's Growth and Managed tiers cover that specifically, at a fraction of an enterprise SEO suite's price.
