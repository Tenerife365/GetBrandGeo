● The Honest Version

Where Rankscale genuinely wins

  • Far broader engine coverage — 17+ engines including Google AI Overviews, AI Mode, DeepSeek, Mistral, Grok, and Copilot, well beyond BrandGEO's 5 core engines.
  • Built for agencies — up to 100 brand dashboard slots, white-label reporting, and a REST API for teams managing many client brands from one account.
  • Unlimited seats on every plan, no per-seat charges as your team grows.
  • An established enterprise customer base — named clients include Bosch, UBS, Cartier, and O2 — plus a free trial on the Pro tier.

Where BrandGEO genuinely wins

  • Flat, predictable pricing — no credit pool to monitor. Rankscale's per-query cost varies 8x by engine (Claude vs. ChatGPT), which can make monthly spend harder to forecast as usage grows.
  • A real managed-service tier — Rankscale has no equivalent at any price, self-serve only even on its $780/mo Enterprise plan.
  • Real per-market geo-context — BrandGEO simulates a user's actual location when firing prompts, not just multi-region reporting on top of a single query.
  • A free one-time audit with no signup required, if you just want a baseline first.

Is BrandGEO cheaper than Rankscale?

It depends on usage, because the two are priced differently. Rankscale's Essentials plan starts lower ($20/mo) but on a credit meter — 120 credits covers roughly 480 AI responses at the cheapest engines, less if you track Claude or DeepSeek, which cost more credits per query. BrandGEO Essentials (€99/mo) and Growth (€299/mo) are flat monthly fees with a fixed prompt/engine allowance included, so your bill doesn't move if usage patterns shift month to month.

Does Rankscale offer a managed service like BrandGEO?

No. Rankscale is self-serve software at every tier, including its $780/mo Enterprise plan and its custom plans — you configure search terms, review dashboards, and act on the data yourself, with optional REST API and white-label reporting for agencies. BrandGEO offers that same self-serve path on its lower tiers, plus a genuinely managed option on Managed, Pro, and Enterprise.

How does Rankscale's credit-based pricing compare to BrandGEO's flat plans?

Rankscale bills by credits: each AI engine query draws down a shared monthly pool, and the cost varies by engine — ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, and Google AI Mode run about 0.25 credits per query, DeepSeek 1 credit, and Claude 2 credits. That rewards careful engine selection but means your effective cost per brand tracked can shift as you add engines or prompts. BrandGEO's plans are flat: Growth and up include all 5 core engines and a fixed prompt allowance for one predictable monthly price, with no credit math to run before you know what a plan actually gets you.
