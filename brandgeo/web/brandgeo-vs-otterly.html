● The Honest Version

Where Otterly.AI genuinely wins

  • The lowest entry price in the category — $29/mo (Lite) is a real, no-catch starting point for a solo marketer or very small team.
  • Unlimited team members on every plan, including the cheapest tier — useful the moment more than one person needs dashboard access.
  • A genuine API and MCP server on Standard and Premium, for teams that want to pipe data into their own tooling or Looker Studio.
  • An established enterprise customer base (Roche, Opera, Garmin, Avis Budget Group among named clients) and a dedicated agency-partner program.

Where BrandGEO genuinely wins

  • All 5 core engines included standard from Growth up — Otterly bills Claude, Gemini, and Google AI Mode as separate add-ons on every tier, which adds up fast if you want full coverage.
  • A real managed-service tier — Otterly has no equivalent at any price; the alternative is a separate $1,500–5,000+/month agency retainer.
  • One platform across the whole journey — start self-serve on Free or Essentials, move to fully managed on Managed or Pro, without switching vendors or re-adding engines you already paid for elsewhere.
  • A free one-time audit with no signup required, if you just want a baseline before committing to either tool.

Is BrandGEO cheaper than Otterly.AI?

It depends on the tier. Otterly's Lite plan ($29/mo) undercuts every BrandGEO tier on pure entry price. But once you add the engines Otterly charges extra for — Claude, Gemini, and Google AI Mode are all paid add-ons on every Otterly plan, from $9-$149/mo each — a fully-loaded Otterly setup with 5-6 engines can run past BrandGEO Growth (€299/mo, all 5 core engines included with no add-on fees). At the done-for-you end, Otterly has no managed-service tier at any price; BrandGEO Managed starts at €900/month for a fully managed engagement.

Does Otterly.AI include Claude and Gemini by default?

No. Every Otterly plan (Lite, Standard, Premium) tracks 4 engines by default — ChatGPT, Google AI Overviews, Perplexity, and Microsoft Copilot. Claude, Gemini, and Google AI Mode are all separate paid add-ons, priced per tier. BrandGEO's Growth tier and above include all 5 core engines — ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, Perplexity, and Meta AI — as standard, with no per-engine add-on pricing to track.

Does Otterly offer a managed or done-for-you service like BrandGEO?

No. Otterly.AI is self-serve software at every tier, including Enterprise — you configure prompts, read the dashboard, and act on the recommendations yourself. BrandGEO offers that same self-serve path on Free, Essentials, and Growth, plus a genuinely managed option on Managed, Pro, and Enterprise, where BrandGEO's team handles strategy, prompt research, and reporting for you.
