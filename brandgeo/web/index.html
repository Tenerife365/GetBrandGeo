FAQ

Questions, answered.

What is GEO (Generative Engine Optimization)?

GEO is the practice of measuring and improving how your brand appears in answers from AI engines like ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, Perplexity and Meta AI. It's the new layer where customers get recommendations before they ever reach a website. It’s what SEO was for Google, applied to AI answers.

Which AI engines does BrandGEO monitor?

Five: ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, Perplexity and Meta AI, all queried with live web search enabled, every week, using the exact prompts your customers actually ask.

How is the AI Visibility Score calculated?

It’s a single 0 to 100 number across six dimensions: recognition, knowledge, sentiment, accuracy, reach and consistency. You can track the trend week over week and drill into any engine or dimension.

How quickly do I get results?

Your free audit lands within 48 hours: a full score, a per-engine breakdown, and a prioritised P0/P1/P2 fix list you can act on straight away.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. The self-serve software plans (Free, Essentials, Growth, Growth PRO) can be upgraded, downgraded or cancelled anytime, with no lock-in and no credit card required to start.

Do you offer a done-for-you option?

Yes. Our managed plans run it for you end to end: strategy, competitor research, reporting and regular calls included, so your team doesn’t have to touch the dashboard unless they want to.
