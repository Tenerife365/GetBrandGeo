● The Honest Version

Where Profound genuinely wins

  • Broadest engine coverage in the category — 10+ AI engines including Copilot, Grok, DeepSeek, Google AI Mode, and AI Overviews, well beyond the 5 core engines most competitors, including BrandGEO, track as standard.
  • Real enterprise scale and credibility — a $1B valuation, 700+ enterprise customers, and 10% of the Fortune 500 as of early 2026.
  • Deep native integrations — GA4 conversion tracking, ChatGPT Shopping visibility, and real-time crawler monitoring across GPTBot, PerplexityBot, and ClaudeBot.
  • Built for a formal enterprise buying process — if you already run procurement and want a dedicated account relationship rather than a self-serve dashboard, that's exactly what Profound is set up for.

Where BrandGEO genuinely wins

  • Published, fixed pricing at every tier — you can see the exact cost of every plan without booking a demo call.
  • A real managed-service tier starting at €900/month — Profound has no managed-service equivalent at any price; its closest comparison is its own $2,000–5,000+/month enterprise tier, which is still self-serve software, not a done-for-you service.
  • Multi-language and geo-context depth built in — including per-market context injection and a dedicated Romanian sentiment lexicon, useful for brands operating outside English-first, US-centric markets.
  • An accessible on-ramp for SMB and mid-market brands that want to start measuring today, not after a sales cycle.

Is Profound better for enterprise brands?

For large, Fortune-500-scale organizations that need the broadest possible engine coverage (10+ engines including Copilot, Grok, and DeepSeek) and already run a formal procurement process, Profound is a strong fit — it's built specifically for that scale, backed by a $1B valuation and 700+ enterprise customers as of early 2026. For brands below that scale, its sales-led, custom-quote-only pricing (third-party estimates put real enterprise deployments at roughly $2,000–5,000+/month) can be a real barrier before you even see a number.

Does BrandGEO track as many AI engines as Profound?

No. Profound tracks 10+ engines — the broadest coverage in the category, including Copilot, Grok, DeepSeek, Google AI Mode, and AI Overviews. BrandGEO tracks 5 core engines as standard from Growth up — ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, Perplexity, and Meta AI — which cover the large majority of real-world commercial query volume for most brands. If you specifically need Copilot, Grok, DeepSeek, or Google AI Mode tracked today, Profound currently covers ground BrandGEO doesn't.

Why doesn't Profound publish its pricing?

Profound's official pricing page is demo-led — it routes buyers to "Get a Demo" rather than a public plan ladder. Third-party sources (G2, independent review sites) cite historical Starter pricing around $99/month and Growth around $399/month, but these aren't confirmed as current live checkout prices. This is typical of an enterprise sales-led motion — BrandGEO's pricing, by contrast, is published and fixed at every tier, so you don't need a sales call to find out what something costs.
