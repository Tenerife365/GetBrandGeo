Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026  ·  BrandGEO / getbrandgeo.com

🔒 BrandGEO is based in the European Union (Santa Cruz de Tenerife, Canary Islands, Spain) and is fully committed to compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR — EU 2016/679).

1. Who We Are

BrandGEO ("we", "us", "our") is an AI Visibility Intelligence service operating under the domain getbrandgeo.com, based in Santa Cruz de Tenerife, Canary Islands, Spain (European Union).

Contact: contact@getbrandgeo.com

2. What Data We Collect

We collect only the data you voluntarily provide through our contact/audit request form:

  • Full name
  • Business email address
  • Company or brand name
  • Industry
  • Optional message / notes

We do not collect sensitive personal data, payment information, or data from minors.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to:

  • Respond to your audit request or enquiry
  • Deliver the free AI visibility audit you requested
  • Follow up regarding BrandGEO services if you expressed interest

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process your data on the basis of legitimate interest (responding to a service enquiry you initiated) and, where applicable, consent (when you opt in to further communication).

5. Data Retention

We apply the following retention periods:

  • Contact enquiries — retained for 24 months from the date of enquiry, or until you request deletion, whichever comes first.
  • AI visibility results (ai_results) — retained for 24 months from the date of collection, then automatically and permanently deleted. This allows clients to analyse 2 years of brand visibility trends while ensuring data does not accumulate indefinitely.
  • Client account data — retained for the duration of the active subscription plus 90 days after termination, to allow for data export. Deleted permanently thereafter upon written request.
  • User accounts — deleted upon written request to hello@getbrandgeo.com.

Retention is enforced automatically. You may request early deletion of your data at any time.

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate our platform:

  • FormSubmit.co — processes contact form submissions (no data stored beyond email delivery)
  • Supabase — database and authentication for the dashboard (EU-compatible, data stored in secure cloud infrastructure)
  • Netlify — hosting for the dashboard (app.getbrandgeo.com)

7. Cookies & Analytics

The getbrandgeo.com website uses Plausible Analytics, a privacy-friendly analytics tool that does not use cookies, does not collect personal data, and does not track visitors across sites. Plausible processes aggregate traffic data (page views, referrer, country, device type) without storing any personally identifiable information. No cookie consent is required. You can review Plausible's data policy at plausible.io/data-policy.

The dashboard (app.getbrandgeo.com) uses only essential session cookies required for authentication. No advertising or third-party tracking cookies are used on any BrandGEO property.

8. Your Rights Under GDPR

As a data subject in the EU, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Erasure — request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restriction — request that we limit how we process your data
  • Portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interest

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at contact@getbrandgeo.com. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact & Complaints

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at contact@getbrandgeo.com.

If you believe we have not handled your data correctly, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. In Spain, this is the Agencia Española de Protección de Datos (AEPD).
