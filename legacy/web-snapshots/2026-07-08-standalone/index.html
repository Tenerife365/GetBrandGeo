Engine by engine

Every AI engine sources its answers differently.

That's why a brand can rank first in one engine and be completely absent in another — and why AI visibility isn't one score, it's several.

ChatGPT

Live citations lean heavily on Bing's search index, alongside OpenAI's own training data.

Gemini

Grounds its answers directly in the Google Search index — the same one that powers traditional rankings.

Perplexity

Combines its own web index with real-time retrieval, weighted toward recent, well-structured content.

Claude

Uses Brave Search for live retrieval, alongside broad training-data recall from sources across the open web.

Microsoft Copilot

Built on Bing's index — closely related to, but not identical to, what drives ChatGPT's citations.
