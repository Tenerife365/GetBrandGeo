## AlternativeTo listing

AlternativeTo works by positioning your product as an alternative *to*
something people already search for. List BrandGEO as an alternative under:

- **Semrush AI Toolkit** (also frame as "alternative to")
- **Ahrefs Brand Radar**
- **Otterly.AI**
- **Profound**
- **Peec AI**

**"Also known as" tags to add:** AI Visibility, GEO monitoring, Generative
Engine Optimization, AI search tracking, brand mention tracking.

**Description (AlternativeTo's format, ~200–300 chars):**

> BrandGEO tracks how your brand appears across ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude,
> Perplexity, and Meta AI — mention rate, sentiment, ranking position, and
> named competitors — with both self-serve software and a fully managed
> service tier. Based in Spain, built for worldwide brands.

**Note on honesty here:** AlternativeTo lets users mark whether a listed
alternative is a "genuine" comparison — since the comparison pages already
published on getbrandgeo.com (`brandgeo-vs-peec.html`, `brandgeo-vs-
profound.html`, etc.) already do honest side-by-side comparisons with
Peec/Profound's genuine strengths stated plainly, this listing can safely
link to those existing pages rather than writing new comparison copy from
scratch.
