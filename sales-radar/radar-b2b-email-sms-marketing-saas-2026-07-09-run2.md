- **Vertical fit (15/15).**
- **Budget/timing (10/15):** Bootstrapped, no funding round, but a real recent growth-milestone press moment (Feb 2026, "$100M+ in merchant-generated revenue") — a genuine trigger, just not a funding event.
- **Reachability (7/10):** Founder Kyle Bigley identified (active on LinkedIn/X), plus a named Customer Success Manager publicly visible — reachable, though not as immediate as a 2-person team.
