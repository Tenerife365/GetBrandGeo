### 1. Privy — 87 (Hot)
- **AI-visibility gap (30/40):** Ran Shopify-popup and email+SMS-combo comparison searches. - **Budget/timing (15/15):** Two acquisitions in the trailing 12 months is a strong double-trigger — Emotive (~12 months ago) and Sendlane (~5.5 months ago, well within window), plus a stated 300% revenue growth figure and "6,000+ ecommerce brands" scale claim.
